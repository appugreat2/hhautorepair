What We Do

Our Services

Expert vehicle care from a team you can trust. No jargon, no surprise bills — just quality work done right.

01

MOT Testing

DVSA-authorised MOT station for Class 4, 5 & 7 vehicles. We give you an honest result and competitive retest pricing if needed.

  • Classes 4, 5 and 7
  • Competitive pricing
  • Same-day retests available
02

Mechanical Repairs

Brakes, clutch, suspension, exhausts, cam belts, full servicing and more — carried out to manufacturer standards across all makes.

  • Brakes & suspension
  • Engine & gearbox work
  • Cam belts & servicing
03

Bodywork & Dent Repair

Panel beating, accident repair, paintwork refinishing and paintless dent removal (PDR) to restore your vehicle to its original condition.

  • Accident & insurance repairs
  • Paintless dent removal
  • Full respray & paintwork

Advanced Diagnostics

Warning light on your dashboard? Our state-of-the-art OBD diagnostic tools pinpoint the exact fault — no guesswork, no wasted money.

  • Engine & ABS fault codes
  • Airbag & DPF diagnostics
  • All makes & models
