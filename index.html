What We Do

Our Services

From your annual MOT to complex bodywork repairs, we cover every aspect of vehicle care with precision and transparency.

01

MOT Testing

Official DVSA-authorised MOT testing for Class 4, 5 and 7 vehicles. Fast turnaround with honest, transparent results.

02

Mechanical Repairs

Brakes, suspension, clutch, engine work and full servicing — all makes and models handled by experienced technicians.

03

Bodywork & Dent Repair

Accident repair, panel beating, paintwork and PDR (paintless dent removal) to restore your vehicle to showroom condition.

04

Advanced Diagnostics

State-of-the-art OBD diagnostics to pinpoint warning lights and hidden faults — so you only pay to fix what actually needs fixing.
